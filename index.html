OUR CERTIFICATE

Experience success at EL KENDY BTS CENTER. Our two-year Higher Technician Certificate program offers specialized knowledge and practical skills. Graduates receive a recognized credential, unlocking diverse professional opportunities. Join us for a prosperous future.

School Events

ECONOMY APS

APS EVENT is an extraordinary gathering where students of CG showcase their application of accounting principles. This captivating event highlights their practical skills in the field of finance and accounting. Through engaging activities and real-life scenarios, students demonstrate their expertise and creativity in managing financial transactions, analyzing data, and making informed business decisions. APS EVENT is a platform where accounting comes to life, inspiring future accountants and fostering a deeper understanding of the dynamic world of finance.

Ramadan initiative

The Ramadan Initiative is a collaborative event where teachers and students come together to provide Ramadan groceries to those in need during the holy month of Ramadan. This heartfelt initiative aims to support and uplift the underprivileged by ensuring they have essential food supplies for their iftar meals. Through the collective efforts of our compassionate educators and students, we strive to spread joy, kindness, and solidarity within our community during this auspicious time. Join us in making a meaningful difference and fostering the spirit of giving during Ramadan.

ISMALA and IMA visit

The visit of BTS Kindy Center by the IMA and ISMALA Institutes Committee for partnership in the field of Artificial Intelligence (AI) is an exciting event. This visit aims to explore potential collaborations between our center and the esteemed institutes in the field of AI. The committee members will have the opportunity to witness our state-of-the-art facilities, cutting-edge technology, and innovative AI programs. Together, we aim to establish a fruitful partnership that fosters advancements in AI education and research, empowering students to excel in this rapidly evolving field.

Posters Day Event

The AI Posters Day event was a remarkable gathering where attendees witnessed a captivating display of cutting-edge research and projects in the field of Artificial Intelligence. Through visually compelling posters, participants explored innovative ideas and advancements in AI, engaging in insightful discussions and fostering collaboration. The event left a lasting impression, inspiring attendees to further contribute to the transformative potential of AI in various industries and sectors.

Progamming Competition

The Programming Competition is an exhilarating event that brings together talented programmers and coding enthusiasts to showcase their skills and problem-solving abilities. Participants engage in intense coding challenges, solving algorithmic problems and tackling real-world scenarios within a limited time frame. This highly competitive environment fosters collaboration, critical thinking, and creativity, pushing participants to their limits. The Programming Competition is a platform where innovation thrives, camaraderie is forged, and the next generation of coding leaders emerges.

Women Day

The Women's Day celebration at our institution is a special event dedicated to honoring and recognizing the invaluable contributions of our female teachers. This empowering occasion aims to highlight their dedication, expertise, and unwavering commitment to education. Through various activities and heartfelt tributes, we celebrate their achievements, leadership, and positive impact on students' lives. The Women's Day event serves as a reminder of the important role that female educators play in shaping the future and inspires everyone to value and support gender equality in the field of education.

AI Workshops

The AI Workshops Day is a dynamic event designed to explore the fascinating realms of Robotics, Machine Learning with TensorFlow, and Emotional Analysis. Participants will delve into the exciting world of AI through interactive workshops led by experts in the field. They will have the opportunity to delve into the intricacies of robotics, understand the power of machine learning with TensorFlow, and explore the nuances of emotional analysis using AI technologies. This immersive event promises to provide valuable insights, hands-on experiences, and practical knowledge, equipping attendees with the skills to harness the potential of AI in these diverse domains.

Female Basketball Competition

The Female Basketball Competition is an electrifying event that celebrates the skill, athleticism, and teamwork of female basketball players. Participants showcase their talent on the court, demonstrating their passion for the sport and their dedication to honing their basketball abilities. The competition fosters a sense of camaraderie, sportsmanship, and empowerment among the participants, inspiring female athletes to excel and shatter barriers in the world of basketball. This thrilling event not only showcases the talent of female players but also encourages inclusivity and highlights the immense potential of women in sports.
